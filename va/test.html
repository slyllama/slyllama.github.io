About the Archives

Greetings, friend, and welcome to the Vosdence Archives!

Many moons ago, a merchant ship erroneously sailed through an Eldritch inter-planar portal, and thus stumbled upon the untouched island of Vosdence. What followed was a long and messy history of habitation, political turmoil, and supernatural activity – and those stories are now being collected and archived here. You’ll find letters, technical drawings, sketches, data, and commentary that have been curated and inter-linked, so that together we can uncover the people, stories, and mysteries which collectively form the Lay of Vosdence.

What the Archives Are

The Vosdence Archives represent a different kind of story-telling experiment in which our world is described through in-world documents that yield nodes – objects, events, and other instances – which are networked together to form a living world of relational aspects.
