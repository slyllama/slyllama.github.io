Slyllama

Fragments of One

Amidst the rocky pools of silver mist,
gentle boots sit steady on sound plateaus.
Pausing, head raised; lantern elevated as platinum
is cast in veins through weathered fog.
At his feet streak wispy orbs of light, chirping
and streaming and bouncing and laughing—
and so he laughs in harmony, chest softly shaking
as surrounding sprouts soak up golden rain.
