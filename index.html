Heyo! I’m Alec, a graphic designer from New Zealand currently producing drawings and documentation for Pipeline Industries. I’m also a project assistant for Kattabolt, and I illustrate for freelance projects like the ‘See You, Space Cowboy’ rulebook. I focus especially on spatial and mechanical visualisation – fictional concepting, technical drawing, and maths-heavy typographic setting, as you can see below. If you’re interested, I’ve got some writing about my design and ideas and processes in the site journal; and you can find some more art-oriented work over on Artstation.

I deeply enjoy the process of game development – especially building, modeling, and configuring the worlds and environments in those games. It’s something I spent most of my free time on (outside of playing Guild Wars 2 with friends, hehe). My hobby reel is below, and I keep my experimental work (with a Github link!) here. Thank you so much for taking a look.
