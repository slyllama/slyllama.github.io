Jade Spring: Development Notes #3

Hey there everyone! Here’s a super-speedy March summary for Jade Spring. (How is it already March…) Last week I put out a quick video tour of the game , which you can check out below. (That’s why these development notes are a bit late – I thought I’d focus on putting the tour together first.) I’m so grateful that people have been watching and enjoying it!

Lots and lots of additions and improvements have gone into the game even since the tour – I’m trying to get things as intuitive, bug-free, and polished as possible. The problems that crop up as a result of competing mechanics and systems can also get pretty spicy*, so I’ve been addressing many of those.

  • Implemented the Attenuator, which allows you to play Elder Dragon melodies on a piano keyboard in order to attune Dragonvoid-dispersing flux.
  • Improved decoration pane layout; ability to search for decorations.
  • Added New Kaineng City floors and stairs, rustic door, Janthiri bee, and more decorations and components.
  • Ability to take screenshots in-game.
  • Many bug fixes i.e., you can no longer ‘lose’ a decoration out of bounds.
  • Added Cid.
 

And lastly, the Steam page will be going up next Monday the 17th! You’ll be able to wishlist it and stuff, and eventually add it to your library for free once it’s out. I know it’s a small deal for a tiny fan game, but I’ve never done that kinda thing before nor gotten nearly this far, so I’m quite anxious but also very excited to have made it to this point. Thank you from my heart to everyone who’s followed the development of Jade Spring and been so supportive of the adventure.
