“Root of All Love” is a short Lovecraftian puzzle experience about monsters and love. It was made in one month for the Fun Baroo “The New Blood – Call of Cthulhu” monster-designing event.

Download b0.4

Ver. b0.4 provides an important fix for Alt-Tab behaviour, alongside other minor tweaks and cosmetic improvements.

This private release version is a work-in-progress, and major improvements and additions are to be expected before a public release! Please report any bugs and let me know what you thought of the game.
