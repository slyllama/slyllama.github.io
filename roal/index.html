“Root of All Love” is a short Lovecraftian puzzle experience about monsters and love. It was made in one month for the Fun Baroo “The New Blood – Call of Cthulhu” monster-designing event.

Download b0.3

This private release version, b0.x, is a work-in-progress, and major improvements and additions are to be expected before a public release! Please report any bugs and let me know what you thought of the game.
