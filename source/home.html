Heyo! o kama pona! I’m Alec, a technical drafter from New Zealand, and a hobbyist game developer. I’m also a project assistant for Kattabolt, and I illustrate for freelance projects like the ‘See You, Space Cowboy’ rulebook. I focus especially on spatial and mechanical visualisation – fictional concepting, technical drawing, and maths-heavy typographic setting, as you can see below. If you’re interested, I’ve got some writing about my design and ideas and processes in the site journal; and you can find some more art-oriented work over on Artstation.

In addition to my graphic work I also enjoy the process of game development – especially building, modeling, and configuring the worlds and environments in those games. It’s something I’ve spent most of my free time on (outside of playing Guild Wars 2 with friends, hehe). mi kama sona e toki-pona. You can also check out my hobby reel below!
