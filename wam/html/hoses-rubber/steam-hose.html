Steam Hose - Roiter WAM

Suitable for conveying saturated steam and hot water.

  • Standard: BS 5342:1985 2A.
  • Temperature range: −35C − 232°C (saturated steam); 95°C (hot water).
  • Safety factor: steam: 10:1; hot water: 3.15:1.
  • Coil lengths: 40m.
  • Pressure: 18bar (sat. steam); 55bar (hot water).
